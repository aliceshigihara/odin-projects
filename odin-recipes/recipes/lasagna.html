Lasagna Recipe

Description

Delicious homemade lasagna with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    •

    Steps

  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  • In a large skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add garlic, basil, salt, and pepper. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and more meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  • Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  • Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
